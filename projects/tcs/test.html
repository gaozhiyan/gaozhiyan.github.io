题目要求:
地球是我们的家园，好好保护地球是我们每个人的责任。作为中学生，我们应该怎样做？请写一篇短文，内容包括 1. 节约用水 给出2点理由 2. 节约纸张 列出实例说明 3. 节约粮食 说说其意义
正文：

The earth is our home. It’s our duty to protect it well. As middle school students, what should we do? First, we should save water. As we know, all the living things can’t live without water. As the pollution is getting more and more serious, the clean water on earth is becoming less and less.

Second, it’s necessary for us to save paper. All the used textbooks, books and newspapers should be recycled. In this way, fewer trees will be cut down. Third, I think we should not waste food. Don’t throw away rice and dishes which we can’t finish. Saving food means reducing the land used. Then we can grow more plants.
